02  REPORT

Stations consistently below 80% across all uploaded files

Only stations that appear in every uploaded file, with every reading under 80%, are shown. A single missing file or a single reading of 80% or higher excludes the station entirely — this isolates stations that have stayed low the whole time, not ones that dipped and recovered.
